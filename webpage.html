CHENNAI INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Chennai Institute of Technology (CIT Chennai) is an Industry Connected Institute, affiliated to Anna University and is a co-educational engineering college located at Kundrathur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It was established in 2010 by the Parthasarathy Seeniammal Educational Trust. Chennai Institute of Technology, the top second college in Tamil Nadu was established with an objective of providing quality technical education with adequate industrial exposure than any other college in Chennai. Chennai Institute of Technology has one of the if not 'THE' best faculties who not only clarify students doubt at any time but also helps and motivates students to reach their dreams.
